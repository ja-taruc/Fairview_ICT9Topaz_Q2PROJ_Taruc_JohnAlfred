How to Brew Barako Coffee

A strong and healthy source of caffeine using Cordillera raised beans, while being in the sunny cold morning of Baguio.

Preparation time

  • Total: 15 Minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Ground Barako Coffee
  • Water
  • Optional: Creamer and Sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil Water: In a coffee pot, fill it with water and bring to boil. Take it off the heat source before it boils
  2. Place the beans into the water Put 2 tablespoons of Barako Coffee Grounds into the water and put on the lid of the pot.
  3. Brew the coffee: Let the coffee grounds brew for 3-5 minutes. Then serve.
  4. Enjoy: Serve hot, add creamer or sugar if you want but it is recommended to enjoy the drink without it.
