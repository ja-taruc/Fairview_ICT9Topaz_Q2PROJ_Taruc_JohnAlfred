How to make E-Tag

A piece of pork which is preserved and dried out, later on is eaten as a snack or "pulutan", E-Tag means to traditionally preserve pork.

Preparation time

  • Total: 1 week
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 Week

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Pork Belly
  • 1 Cup of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons of Ground Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the meat: Remove the excess fat of the meat leaving atleast 3 centimeters of fat for moisture.
  2. Season the meat: Spread and massage the salt and pepper on the meat
  3. Curing stage: Hang the meat onto and hanger and secure it properly and let it dry in the sun, or you can put it on your roof the traditional way.
  4. Serving the meat: After a week of curing, check the meat for maggots or worms (it is normal), cut the meat into uniform 2 inch pieces, you can grill it or boil it with the pinikpikan.
  5. Enjoy: Serve on a plate or bowl with the pinikpikan, enjoy and drink it with Rice Wine.
